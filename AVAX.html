What is Avalanche?

Avalanche is a smart contract blockchain platform. Developers can deploy decentralized applications such as decentralized finance, gaming, NFTs, and much more. Validators secure the network through a proof-of-stake consensus protocol which allows transactions to be processed fast at a low cost and environmental friendly manner. The platform has grown to secure over 100+ individual projects, $1.4M+ of AVAX burned (reducing supply), 950+ individual block-producing validators, and over 500k+ community members around the globe.
